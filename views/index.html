Balderdash - Player

How to play Balderdash:

  1. You will be asked a question by the quiz master. Please don't answer the question until asked to do so by the quiz master.
  2. At random someone (the 'dasher') will be given the correct answer. This person must acknowledge that they have the answer to the quiz master without letting anyone else know.
  3. You can now answer the question. The person who has the correct answer must enter the correct answer (they can make it seem like a lie by embellishing, but must provide the correct answer), everyone else must make up a believable answer, or answer correctly if they want.
  4. When everyone has answered, the quiz master will ask everyone in turn to pick the number of the answer that they believe is correct.
  5. After everyone has answered, the quiz master will award points
    1. If someone chooses your answer and you are not the 'dasher', you get one point
    2. If you get the correct answer, you get two points (unless you are the 'dasher')
    3. The 'dasher' gets 3 points if no one chooses your answer
    4. The 'dasher' gets 1 point if one person chooses your answer
  6. There are 10 questions, so not every person in the game may get to be the dasher.
  7. The scores will be announced at the end of each round.
  8. The winner is the player (or players in the event of a tie) who have the most points.
  9. The game works better with a few drinks to hand!

Here are some tips to make the game a bit more playable...
  • Please refresh your web page to get the question (when prompted to do so by the quiz master)
  • The page doesn't automatically refresh... when people are answering, you can refresh your page to see answers. Only the quiz master can see who has made a particular suggestion.
  • Please only submit one answer. If you get it wrong, just live with it. It helps to check what you are about to submit before you do so. This will just save tons of confusion.
